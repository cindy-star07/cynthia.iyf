Pamoja Foundation Kenya

Pamoja Foundation Kenya was founded in 2017 by a group of community youth in Nairobi,Kenya.

We are a charitable organization with a special focus on changing lives of children,youth and women in slums. It is dedicated to making a difference in the community through various initiatives and programs.

We are committed to fostering sustainable development,education and healthcare for all people in need.


About Us

Mission Statement: Our mission is to provide support and resources to those in need.

Vision: To build our country a place where every individual has access to essential resources and opportunities.

Values: Compassion, Integrity, Transparency.

Our Services

Educational Programs


Scholarships:We offer financial assistance to the less unfortunate children to help them pay their tuition.

Special Needs Education:Providing educational support for individuals with disabilities.

After-School Programs: Offering academic support, extracurricular activities, and mentorship for children and adolescents..

Direct Assistance Programs


Disaster Relief:Providing immediate assistance to individuals and families affected by natural disasters such as floods,drought,fire oubreak and many more.

Medical Assistance:We offer free clinic days in basic healthcare services to low-income individuals and families.

Menstrual Hygiene Products: We contribute to the overall health and well-being of communities, particularly in areas with limited access to sanitory towels by helping women and girls maintain their dignity and hygiene during their menstrual periods

Home care services


Shelter Options:We Consider options such as temporary shelters, transitional housing, or permanent housing for the less fortunate

Food Programs: Implement programs like food banks, meal distribution, or community kitchens.
